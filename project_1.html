1. General Information about the Project

The online school platform is being created to provide modern and interactive English language learning opportunities. It connects students seeking to improve their skills with professional teachers offering high-quality services.

The goal is to create an intuitive service that meets modern users' needs, emphasizing the integration of advanced technologies such as automated teacher matching, student progress analytics, and a convenient, attractive design.

Start Date: December 21, 2024
End Date: December 21, 2025
Project Manager: Manager 1
Budget: $30,000

2. Main Functions of the Platform

  • User Registration: Students create profiles indicating their proficiency level and learning goals. Teachers provide profiles with details on qualifications, experience, schedules, and rates.
  • Matching Students and Teachers: The platform matches teachers with students based on criteria like difficulty level, preferred schedule, or specific topics.
  • Lesson Booking: Integrated calendar for scheduling lessons.
  • Payments and Distribution: Secure payment system for lessons, with automated earnings distribution.
  • Reviews and Ratings: Users can leave feedback to improve trust and learning quality.

3. Main Goals of the Project

  • Short-Term Goals:
    • Enter the top 3 platforms.
    • Ensure a balanced ratio between students and teachers.
    • Register 100 teachers and 1,000 students in the first year.
    • Achieve a 90% satisfaction rate by the end of the first year.
    • Create a fast and user-friendly platform.
  • Long-Term Goals:
    • Become the top platform in Ukraine.
    • Maintain highly rated teachers with scores above 4.5.
    • Register 1,000 teachers and 10,000 students.
    • Grow social media followers to 10,000+.
    • Achieve an annual turnover of 10,000,000 UAH.

4. Main Functional Features

  • Registration System: Personalized profiles for students and detailed teacher profiles.
  • Lesson Booking: Calendar integration and lesson notifications.
  • Payment Processing: Secure gateway and earnings distribution.
  • Ratings and Feedback: Student ratings for teachers and feedback opportunities.
  • Support System: Chat and email support for technical and service issues.

5. Technical Details

  • Frontend: React.js-based adaptive design.
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.
  • Database: MongoDB for scalable data storage.
  • Payment Gateway: Stripe or PayPal integration.
  • Deployment: Hosting on AWS or similar platforms.

6. Team Structure

  • Project Manager: Oversees project execution.
  • Product Manager: Aligns goals with market and customer needs.
  • Developers: Work on frontend, backend, and database architecture.
  • Test Engineers: Ensure platform functionality.
  • UI/UX Designer: Creates intuitive and user-friendly design.
  • Support Team: Assists users with queries and issues.

7. Timeline

  • Phase 1 (0-3 months): Requirement gathering and initial design.
  • Phase 2 (4-6 months): Development of core features and payment gateway integration.
  • Phase 3 (7-9 months): Testing, bug fixes, and beta release.
  • Phase 4 (10-12 months): Full launch and marketing campaigns.

8. Marketing and Promotion

  • Collaboration with educational institutions.
  • Initial discounts for early users.
  • Advertisements via bloggers.
  • Setting up organic traffic.

10. Risks and Mitigation

  • Low User Engagement: Regular updates and feedback collection.
  • Technical Issues: Reliable server architecture and support team.
  • Payment Problems: Secure gateways and transaction monitoring.

11. Evaluation and Success
